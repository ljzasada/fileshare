StickyWheels v0.2
by khorio
---------------------------------------
StickyWheels is a RagePluginHook plugin that keeps the wheels turned
the way you left them when stationary.
When you leave the vehicle the wheels will retain their angle,
entering the vehicle will not straighten them out again.

Requirements: a recent copy of RagePluginHook

Usage:
- Turn the wheel left or right when stationary
