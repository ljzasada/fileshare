KTFDO - Keep The F****** Door Open v0.1
by khorio
---------------------------------------
KTFDO is a RagePluginHook plugin that stops the door from closing
every time you leave your vehicle.

Requirements: a recent copy of RagePluginHook

Usage:
- Open and close door normally: Tap the leave vehicle key once
- Open door and leave open: Hold down the leave vehicle key
